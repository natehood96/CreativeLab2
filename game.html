Race to Pallet Town

You're a Pokemon on a mission: getting to Pallet Town. You've got a fast car, but the road is unpredictable and filled with trainers who are out to get you.

Use the left and right arrow keys to drive side to side. Make sure you stay on the path and avoid the trainers trying to stop you!

As you level up along the way, the game will become more difficult.

Once you reach Pallet Town, keep playing to see how long you can survive.

Press "p" at any time to pause the game, and "r" to resume.
